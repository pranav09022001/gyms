+ Balance

BALANCE


Type: Cardio / Aerobic / Stress Relief / Flexibility

Location: Downstairs Studio

Calories Busted: Up to 300 per class

Class Level: Beginner to Advanced

Balance classes are something a little different here at Royal Fitness. Along with improving overall fitness levels, the class focuses more on mind cantering and proper breathing techniques.
Classes last 55 minutes per session and begin with a tai chi and Pilates warm up segment of flowing moves. Next up we include a series of yoga movements to get things going followed by stretching and balance moves.
Once part one is complete the class moves on to the secondary segment which includes slow twists and bends to fully engage your core muscles. Lastly the class finishes off with meditation and observation of the effects of the class on your body and mind.
The aim is to improve your cardiovascular fitness, reduce your stress levels and improve your body’s flexibility. It’s the perfect class for those living a stressful life and needs to unwind while also improving their fitness.

What to wear: Normal gym clothing will do just fine
What to bring: At least one bottle of water and a towel

+ Body Combat

BODY COMBAT


Type: Cardio / Aerobic / Full Body Toning

Location: Downstairs Studio

Calories Busted: Up to 740 per class

Class Level: Beginner to Advanced

Gender: Male & Female

BODYCOMBAT is a fun, empowering cardio workout where you will undergo a cracking workout, whilst relieving stress and raising your heart rate. This energetic class, available throughout our Fitness & Wellbeing estate, is strongly influenced by martial arts such as Karate, Boxing, Taekwondo, Tai Chi and Muay Thai. The classes are supported by powerful music and energetic instructors. You will punch, kick and kata your way through calories to a leaner, healthier, fitter body. The classes can be explosive, so remember to pace yourself.

+ Body Pump

BODY PUMP


Type: Cardio / Aerobic / Resistance / Light Weights / Full Body Toning Location: Downstairs Studio

Calories Busted: Up to 540 per class

Class Level: Beginner to Advanced

Gender: Male & Female

If you’ve ever been to a gym before there’s a good chance you will have heard of Body Pump. From New Zealand based fitness masters Les Mills, it’s a class that has been around for over a quarter of a century and that staying power is testament to it working for so many people of all ages, sizes and weights across the world. Being a weight based workout and employing the use of a lightweight barbell you will instantly notice the difference in this class compared to a cardio only workout. Not only will you tone your body but your strength will improve dramatically too. Initially conceived as a class that both men and woman would feel equally at home attending, Body Pump has grown into the power house it is today because it works. At between 45 minutes to one hour long, you really get a massive boost to your fitness levels after every class and the results speak for themselves. Each class features 8 separate muscle group specific tracks and incorporate squats, presses, deadlifts and more all with low weights and high repetitions.

What to wear: Normal gym clothing will do just fine
What to bring: At least one bottle of water and a towel

+ Thai Boxing

THAI BOXING


Type: Cardio / Aerobic

Location: Downstairs Studio

Calories Busted: Up to 600 per class

Class Level: Beginner to Advanced

At Royal Fitness we like to offer more than just the normal rota of fitness classes and our Thai Boxing classes are just one example of this. Our resident Thai Boxing instructor Denise Cork has trained for over 10 years and has visited Thailand to further develop her skills, bringing that knowledge back to Scotland where has is involved in competitive fighting competitions. Thai boxing is a combat sport (martial art) that has been developed in Thailand. It is known also as “the art of eight limbs”, because both hands, elbows, knees, and legs can be used. The training involved is known for being strong and firm. It aims to harden the eight members of the body and almost every act of moving in Thai boxing use the whole body, while turning the hip to every kick and every blow with the fist. Give our Thai boxing class a go to increase your lean muscle mass and muscular endurance while improving your stamina, speed and body co-ordination.

What to wear: Normal gym clothing will do just fine
What to bring: At least one bottle of water and a towel

+ Yoga

YOGA


Type: Cardio / Aerobic / Mind

Location: Downstairs Studio

Calories Busted: Up to 200 per class

Class Level: Beginner to Advanced

It wasn’t so long ago that yoga was considered something more of a pass time than a serious fitness tool – but how times have changed! Sports legends like Andy Murray and Ryan Giggs have spoken out recently as advocates of yoga and more and more people are catching the bug and getting involved! You don’t have to be involved in sports to see the pluses either as everyone, of all shapes and sizes can and will find benefit by coming to a class – they key is to make the decision to attend not just one yoga class, but at least 4 to begin to see the changes in body and mind. What you’ll discover is that there are improvements in your flexibility, you’ll get stronger muscles, begin to look better and issues such as back pain and arthritis will be helped too. Your balance will improve along with circulation, breathing and posture too. After a few classes you’ll learn to settle your mind and body leading to better sleep, less stress in your daily life and a sharpened awareness.
